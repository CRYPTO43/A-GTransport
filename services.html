Dry Freight

Our dry van services are necessary when your shipment doesn’t require a temperature-controlled environment but needs to be secured and protected from the elements. This service benefits those shipping goods such as clothing, dry consumables, paper products, small machinery, tools and auto parts.

Reefer Freight(Temperature Controlled)

Our fleet of refrigerated trailers can handle your frozen and refrigerated freight at the temperature you need. State-of-the-art refrigerated trailers enable shippers to have single, dual or triple temperatures within one trailer. We have a modern fleet of Thermo King and Carrier refrigerated trailers with the best temperature recovery times on the market. All of our refrigerated units use ibright® software to monitor your load’s temperature throughout a trip

Machinery Hauling

Our Machinery hauling involves moving heavy equipment or machinery from one location to another. This type of hauling requires specialized equipment and expertise due to the weight and size of the machinery being transported. Examples of machinery that may require hauling include construction equipment, agricultural machinery, industrial machinery, and mining equipment.
